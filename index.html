Bruce Lee Timeline

I fear not the man who has practiced 10,000 kicks once, but I fear the man who has practiced one kick 10,000 times.

Bruce Lee

Lee Jun Fan otherwise known as Bruce Lee was born in San Francisco, USA.

Bruce and his family return to Hong Kong.

Bruce makes his film career at the age of six.

Bruce begins training under the legendary Yip Man.

Bruce wins the British Crown Colony Cha-Cha Championship (a dance contest).

After getting into too many street fights in Hong Kong, Bruce's parents send him back to San Francisco.

Bruce enrolls at the University of Washington and begins teaching students gung fu.

Bruce takes his future wife Linda out on their first date.

Bruce marries Linda; he also establishes a second gung fu school in Oakland.

Bruce's first son Brandon Bruce Lee is born.

Bruce stars in the TV show, "The Green Hornet". He also opens a third gung fu school in Los Angeles.

Bruce's daughter Shannon Lee is born.

Stars in his first major motion picture filmed in Hong Kong titled in the USA, "Fists of Fury".

Stars in his second major motion picture filmed in Hong Kong titled in the USA, "The Chinese Connection".

Stars in his third major motion picture filmed in Italy featuring Chuck Norris titled in the USA, "The Return of the Dragon".

Bruce begins work on another major motion picture featuring Kareem Abdul-Jabbar titled, "Game of Death".

Bruce completes his most successful major motion picture titled, "Enter the Dragon".

Bruce Lee dies in Hong Kong one month before "Enter the Dragon" premieres to the public.

Bruce Lee Hong Kong funeral is attended by over 25,000 people.

Bruce Lee is finally laid to rest near his family in Seattle, USA.

Knowing is not enough; We must apply. Willing is not enough; We must do.

Bruce Lee
